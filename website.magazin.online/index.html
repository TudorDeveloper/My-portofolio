Samsung Galaxy Watch product description

Smartwatch Samsung Galaxy Watch

Samsung offers you a new smartwatch, which will immediately become your favorite gadget.
Samsung Galaxy Watch is a smartwatch, which comes with a lot of surprises and news developed just for you! The 1.3-inch display allows you a clear and easy view of notifications. Due to the size of the screen, you can now answer messages received right from your smartwatch, having the opportunity to do the same with your calls.
The improved microphones of the watch offer you a sound quality at the highest standards, and the entire telephone conversation takes place safely, right from your new device offered by Samsung. Another novelty is the rotating frame of the watch, which allows you to easily go through the menu of the Galaxy Watch smartwatch.
In terms of health, the device is designed to help you achieve your goals. The watch includes a number of 39 sports activities, so your workouts are diverse, but at the same time very effective. In addition to these activities, the watch also has several sensors that manage to provide you with valuable information, for example: strainer to determine the level of stress, barometer, altimeter and heart rate. Regarding your location and orientation during hiking, the included GPS will ensure that you will always be on the right track.
If you want to listen to your favorite songs, now you can connect your smartwatch to bluetooth headphones and enjoy good music stored in the internal memory of the device. Galaxy Watch is water resistant and has a lifespan of up to 4 days with just one charge.
Choose Samsung Galaxy Watch, available on both Android and iOS!

Smartwatch Samsung Galaxy Watch 3

Samsung presents the newest, revolutionary and high-performance smartwatch, Samsung Galaxy Watch 3 Stainless Steel 45mm Mystic Black Black! This smartwatch enjoys the most advanced health monitoring system, available on a smartwatch, managing to combine productivity at the level of smartphones with state-of-the-art health technology. The most advanced Galaxy watch to date, the Galaxy Watch 3 Stainless Steel 45mm Mystic Black Black helps you effortlessly manage your life and health - giving you control over your well-being.
The watch enjoys an imposing design, thought down to the last detail, thus becoming the only smartwatch with a precision rotating frame for navigating the application. With a fine rotating frame and a large screen, it's easier to visualize and focus on what matters. Galaxy Watch3 is thinner, smaller and about 15% lighter than previous models.
Complete the iconic Galaxy Watch3 look with the largest number of watch dials you can choose from, all matching its elegant structure. Premium quality, up to the belt! This is what the new Galaxy Watch3 promises, as it is enriched with a natural leather strap.
Regarding health information, the Samsung Galaxy Watch 3 monitors the following: Your blood oxygen level, using red LEDs and infrared rays to assess your blood oxygen saturation (SpO2) or the amount of oxygen in your bloodstream.
Sleep monitoring is performed through the recording of REM cycles, deep sleep and total sleep time to assess and help improve the quality of rest. Equipped with over 120 home training programs to choose from, Galaxy Watch3 helps you have an active lifestyle, whenever you want, giving you the opportunity to choose from those training programs. Use the Ridge Sport Band for even more accurate statistics.
The running guide allows you to keep up because you receive recommendations when you are on the move. The advanced algorithm analyzes the way you move, to help improve your shape and prevent injuries. You can even measure VO2 max (maximum oxygen uptake) values ​​and have information on daily, weekly and monthly performance, right from your phone.
Besides all these functions, you also enjoy the smart reply, bitmoji, music playback functions through the Spotify and GPS application. The watch is waterproof, being IP68 / 5ATM certified.
Enjoy the newest and smartest smartwatch developed by Samsung!

Smartwatch Samsung Active

The new generation Samsung Galaxy Watch Active proposes a smart fitness watch that, in addition to monitoring information, also offers actively tips so you can maximize the performance of your workouts.
You can match it depending on your lifestyle or mood with a variety of girls who can change. It is also compatible with various models of 20 mm straps.
The "Daily activity" function allows you to achieve your daily health goals by being active, doing sports and sitting down as little as possible. Galaxy Watch Active uses dedicated motion sensors to automatically monitor seven types of activity.
Includes an integrated heart rate monitor, which will send instant alerts when an abnormal heart rate is detected. It can also read stress levels, and you can use the Breath Session function to regain your composure. Check the difference between stress levels before and after breathing exercises.
With the integrated Samsung Health application, Galaxy Watch Active is your personal mentor in terms of health. View a summary of your health in an instant, on a single screen, and access dozens of tools that allow you to manage not only your physical condition, but also your health. * To use the Samsung Health application with the Galaxy Watch Active, you need to register and pair with your compatible smartphone.
It keeps you connected day or night with the ability to send personalized answers using the speech-to-text feature, emoticons or its intuitive keyboard, while the phone charges on the nightstand.
A device that watches over you overnight. Galaxy Watch Active monitors the four stages of sleep so you have a full night's rest. The next morning you will wake up with a clear analysis of the depth of your sleep in the last hours.
It is enough to charge your smartwatch in a few days. With a long battery life in a single charge (up to 45 hours depending on the settings), Galaxy Watch Active can handle various applications (allows Wireless charging and is compatible with the innovative Wireless Power Share feature).
